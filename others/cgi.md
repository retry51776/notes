# CGI

Interaction surface is key variable

## Low Interaction

Examples:

- Distant mountains
- Static building
- Skybox
- Ground planes

## Medium Interaction

Examples:

- Skin
- Cloth
- Hair
- Reflective metal
- Glass metal (transparent)
- Facial animation

Needs:

- Physics or optical modeling
  - Collision detection
  - Deformation models
- Local dependence
  - reflection
  - transparent
  - secondary motion
- Feedback limited

## High Interaction

Examples:

- Water
- Fire
- Smoke
- Explosions
- Mud
- Snow
- Sand

Needs:

- Self interaction
- Continuous time evolution
- Single general force/gradient

## Ultra Interaction

- Crowds
- Wet surfaces

- Multiple independence forces

## Interaction Dimensions

Light interaction
Physical deformation
Physical deformation
Self-interaction
Time continuity
Agent feedback
Perception loop
